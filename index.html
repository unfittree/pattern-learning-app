Fill the blanks

Make your own pattern

Tap pictures to build a pattern. Try to repeat your pattern at least two times.

Simple Materials for Offline Pattern Play

  • Paper strips or index cards, crayons, and stickers for quick AB or AAB cards.
  • Household objects for copy patterns: spoons, toy cars, blocks, socks, or cups.
  • Printable shape cards from PBS for cut-and-place pattern games.
  • Nature finds for outdoor patterns: leaves, rocks, or flowers.

Parent Tips

  • Use short sessions (5-10 minutes), then switch to hands-on materials.
  • Talk out loud together: “red, blue, red, blue...” so she hears the repeat unit.
  • Mix visual patterns with movement patterns like clap-stomp-clap-stomp.
